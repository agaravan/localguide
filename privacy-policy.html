1. Overview

Local Guide is a app that near by people answering that helps user ask questions, submit answers, and follow other people’s questions through app. In a bid to extend its localisation efforts this app connects local questions with local answers. Local Guide allows users to get their neighbourhood-related questions answered on an open chat forum. The app tracks the user’s GPS location to connect them to their neighbour hood after sign-up. Users can be passive consumers too, and simply swipe through the entries without participating. Google believes that with the app, even the biggest of cities can become a lot more “local guide” and familiar to those who do not belong there. It enables a “human, helpful, local way to ask and answer questions” and allows people make their “neighbour hood better, together”.  (each a “Query”).
